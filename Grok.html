Flat Earth Details

The Flat Earth theory posits that the Earth is a flat plane, often depicted as a disk with Antarctica as an ice wall barrier. Proponents argue that evidence like the lack of curvature in photos and the behavior of water supports their view.

Moon Landing Hoax Details

Skeptics claim the moon landing was filmed in a studio, citing inconsistencies like the waving flag, lack of stars in photos, and shadows that don't align with a single light source.

Lizard People Details

This theory suggests that a race of reptilian humanoids secretly controls global affairs, disguising themselves as human leaders. Believers point to supposed physical traits like slit pupils or unusual behavior.
