Moon Landing Hoax

1969NASAPhotos

Claim: The Apollo missions were staged on a soundstage. Evidence cited: anomalies in photos, waving flag, shadows.

Why skeptics disagree

Extensive engineering records, telemetry, lunar samples, and independent tracking by other nations make the hoax claim extremely unlikely.
