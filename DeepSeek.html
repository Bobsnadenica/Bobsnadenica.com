The Moon Landing Hoax

Origin: 1969 Medium Credibility

The theory claims that the Apollo moon landings were faked by NASA and possibly other organizations. Proponents argue that the U.S. government staged the landings to win the Space Race against the Soviet Union at the height of the Cold War.

  • Alleged anomalies in photographs (crosshairs behind objects, inconsistent shadows)
  • Absence of stars in lunar sky photos
  • Waving flag in vacuum of space
  • No blast crater under lunar module

Flat Earth Theory

Origin: Ancient/Revived 19th century Low Credibility

The belief that Earth is a flat disk rather than a globe. Modern flat Earth theories often include the idea of a conspiracy by governments and space agencies to hide this "truth," with NASA being a primary target of suspicion.

  • Horizon appears flat to human eye
  • Antarctica is actually an ice wall surrounding the disk
  • Gravity is an illusion - the disk is accelerating upward
  • All space agencies are part of the conspiracy

Reptilian Elite

Origin: 1990s (David Icke) Low Credibility

The theory that shape-shifting reptilian aliens control Earth by taking on human form and gaining political power. Key figures like world leaders and celebrities are allegedly part of this reptilian race that feeds on human energy or blood.

  • Alleged "slips" where figures' eyes appear reptilian
  • Ancient Sumerian texts describing Anunnaki
  • Common symbolism of reptiles in secret societies
  • Unnatural longevity of certain powerful figures

Chemtrails

Origin: 1990s Medium Credibility

The belief that long-lasting trails left by aircraft are chemical or biological agents deliberately sprayed for purposes undisclosed to the general public. Purposes range from weather modification to population control or mind control.

  • Unusual persistence of contrails compared to historical patterns
  • Alleged finding of barium, strontium in soil samples
  • Patent records for atmospheric delivery systems
  • Increased respiratory illnesses in "spray" areas

Denver International Airport

Origin: 1990s Medium Credibility

The theory that Denver International Airport is the headquarters of the New World Order, with secret underground bunkers and tunnels. The airport's unusual art, murals depicting apocalyptic scenes, and construction delays have fueled speculation.

  • Apocalyptic murals showing mass death and Nazi imagery
  • Time capsule with "New World Airport Commission" plaque
  • Massive underground facilities beyond normal airport needs
  • Strange dedication capstone with Masonic symbols

Hollow Earth

Origin: 17th century/Modern variations Low Credibility

The concept that Earth is entirely hollow or contains a substantial interior space. Some versions propose inner suns, advanced civilizations, or entrances at the poles. Nazis allegedly searched for these entrances during WWII.

  • Alleged Admiral Byrd diaries describing inner Earth
  • Unexplained compass readings near poles
  • Ancient myths of underworld civilizations
  • Satellite images showing possible polar openings
